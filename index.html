Personal project blog

My biggest passion in life has always been mathematics. At 15 I realized I could use math to express my thoughts better than the written word. Wanting to further mature my abilities I received a Bachelors in mathematics. My education gave me the practical skills and the flexibility to be successful in a wide range of fields.

Robotics and control have been the most satisfying applications of my skills. I am left with total excitement and wonder imagining the possibilities of computers which can sense and see the world. A future with intelligent cars aiding their drivers, hardware that can augment human vision and UAVs which gracefully navigate the sky is the future I wish to contribute to. Every project hosted here will be things that I have done, not for school or work, but on behalf of my own curiosity and natural desire to learn more.
